01 Profile

Engineer first.
The rest follows.

I'm Bello Ahmed Oluwatosin — a graduate of Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the University of Lagos, now operating at the intersection of engineering, business, and financial markets.

As Founder and Managing Director of BTech Engineering, I lead a company focused on delivering sustainable, cost-effective energy solutions across Nigeria — from solar systems to smart energy platforms like PowerLink™.

I'm currently pursuing an MSc in Financial Engineering, adding rigorous quantitative and financial modelling tools to my engineering foundation. I also hold a Mini MBA, equipping me to translate technical vision into business strategy.

I've been part of several startups and businesses, bringing both operational and strategic value — from product management to go-to-market execution. I thrive in environments where complex engineering meets capital markets thinking.

Investment Thesis
Nigeria's core engineering challenges — energy, logistics, safety — are not problems to manage. They are investable opportunities waiting for the right builder to unlock them.

I follow the Nigerian equity markets closely and read financial news daily — not just as an investor, but as a builder who understands that attracting capital is as strategic as building the product itself.

My thesis: the next generation of great African businesses will be built by people who can engineer the solution and structure the financing.
